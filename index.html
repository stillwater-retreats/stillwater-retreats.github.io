Amenity Guides

🌡️ Smart Thermostat

The Nest thermostat is located in the living room. Simply tap the screen to adjust temperature. The system learns your preferences automatically. For manual control, turn the outer ring clockwise to increase heat or counterclockwise to cool.

📺 Smart TV & Entertainment

The 65" Samsung smart TV has Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, and more pre-loaded. Use the Samsung remote or voice control by saying "Hi Bixby." The soundbar remote controls volume. Gaming console controllers are in the TV stand drawer.

☕ Coffee Station

Enjoy our premium coffee setup! The Keurig machine uses K-cups (provided). For espresso, use the Nespresso machine with the capsules in the drawer. French press and pour-over supplies are in the cabinet above.

🧺 Washer & Dryer

Located in the utility closet. Washer: Add detergent (provided), select "Normal" cycle, press start. Dryer: Clean lint trap first, select "Auto Dry," press start. Dryer sheets are on the shelf above.

🏊 Pool & Hot Tub

Pool hours: 6 AM - 10 PM. Hot tub: Available 24/7, temperature preset to 102°F. Towels available in the pool house. Please shower before entering. Pool toys are in the storage box.

🔥 Fireplace

Gas fireplace with remote control. Press the "ON" button, then use the flame height buttons to adjust. Automatic shut-off after 4 hours. Remote is on the mantle. Perfect for cozy evenings!

House Rules

🕐 Quiet Hours
Please keep noise to a minimum between 10 PM and 8 AM. This includes music, TV volume, and conversations on balconies or outdoor areas.
🚫 No Smoking
This is a non-smoking property. Smoking is not permitted anywhere inside the house. Designated outdoor smoking area is on the back patio.
🎉 No Parties
No parties or events allowed. Maximum occupancy is 6 guests. Unauthorized guests or parties will result in immediate eviction.
🧹 Checkout Process
Strip beds and place linens in the laundry basket. Load and start the dishwasher. Take out trash. Turn off all lights and lock all doors. Leave keys on the kitchen counter.
🐕 Pet Policy
Sorry, no pets allowed except for registered service animals. Unauthorized pets will incur a $200 cleaning fee.
🚗 Parking
Two parking spaces available in the driveway. Street parking is free but observe posted signs. Do not block neighbors' driveways.
