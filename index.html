House Rules

🕐 Quiet Hours
We are nestled in an amazing community, please respect our neighbors as they respect us. Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM.
🚫 No Smoking Indoors
There is no smoking inside the home. E-cigarettes and vaping included. You may smoke outside. Please make sure to dispose of all leftovers.
🎉 No Parties
At this time we do not allow parties or events.
🐕 Pets Policy
We do allow a maximum of two animals. Make sure you pick up the yard of any droppings before you go.
⚠️ Report Damages
We understand that things do happen but please let us know if anything has been broken or damaged during your stay. This communication allows us ample time to fix or coordinate replacement items with you, if needed.
🧹 Checkout Instructions
Double check that you have all your belongings! Please empty any food from the fridge. Collect any garbage into appropriate trash bins and take out to outside bins. Place used dishes into dishwasher and run it. All used towels can go into the washer and begin wash cycle. Turn all lights off and make sure all windows and doors are shut and locked prior to leaving the home. Adjust thermostat to auto 68.
